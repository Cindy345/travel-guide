Japan

From bustling cities like Japan is a country in Asia known for its rich culture, technology, and delicious food like sushi and ramen. It's made up of islands and has a mix of modern cities and traditional towns.

Top 3 Attractions

1. Mount fuji

Mount Fuji in Japan is a big volcano that's still active. It's around 100 kilometers away from Tokyo. People often call it "Fuji-san". It's the tallest mountain in Japan, standing at 3,776 meters tall.

2. Osaka Castle

Osaka Castle is a big castle in Osaka, Japan. It's in a place called Chūō-ku. This castle is really famous in Japan and a lot of people know about it.

3. Tokyo Tower

The Tokyo Tower is a tall tower in Tokyo, Japan. It's in a part of Tokyo called Shiba-koen, which is in Minato district. They built it in 1958 for communication and to see things from up high.

Famous Cities

1. Tokyo

The capital city of Japan.

2. Osaka

Osaka is a big city by the sea in Japan on a place called Honshu. People like it because it has lots of tall buildings, fun things to do at night, and delicious food you can eat on the streets.

3. Kyoto

Kyoto was Japan's old main city and it's on Honshu island. Lots of people know it for its old Buddhist temples, beautiful gardens, fancy palaces, special Shinto shrines, and old-style wooden houses.
