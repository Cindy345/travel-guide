Korea

Korea, located in East Asia, is a country known for its rich culture, history, and technological advancements.

Top 3 Attractions

1. N Seoul Tower

Korea's first general radio wave tower from 1969. It is completed in 1971.

2. Nationak museum of Korea

National treasures of Korea are stored there. It's home to best collection of history

3. Gyeongbokgung Palace

Gyeongbokgung Palace was the main royal palace long ago for the Joseon dynasty in South Korea. It was built in 1395 and is in the northern part of Seoul, which is a big city in South Korea.
